About Code Like A Girl

Our Mission

The mission of Code Like A Girl is to create an environment conducive to the teaching of technology education to girls and to combat the negative effects of stereotyping that oftentimes preclude girls from seeking an education in technology oriented fields like computer science and engineering. To this end, the organization Code Like A Girl was founded to host classes and workshops that aim to create places where girls can meet like-minded peers and explore their interests in STEM fields.
